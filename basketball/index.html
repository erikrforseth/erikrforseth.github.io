For the past couple of years, a hobby of mine has been writing code to predict the outcomes of college basketball games.

After getting up and running, I began submitting daily picks to the Prediction Tracker under the moniker "ERFunctionRatings," which I've continued to do for this 2015-2016 season. The model's performance has taken a hit this year, but the performance of the other predictors has suffered as well. This may or may not be related to rule changes, or we may simply be in the midst of a generally "noisy" season.

Regardless, I'm often tinkering with this project in my ever-vanishing free time, and have some potential improvements coming down the pike for next season.
