For the past year or so, a hobby of mine has been writing code to predict the outcomes of college basketball games; this evolved partly as an exercise to teach myself some Python and machine learning techniques, and partly as a new way to follow my favorite sport.

After getting up and running last year, I began submitting daily picks to the Prediction Tracker under the moniker "ERFunctionRatings," which I've continued to do for this 2015-2016 season. The model's performance has taken a hit this year, but the performance of the other predictors has suffered as well. This may or may not be related to rule changes, or it may just be a generally "noisy" season this year. Regardless, I'm often tinkering with this project in my ever-vanishing free time, and have some potential improvements coming down the pike for next season.
