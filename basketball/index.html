NCAA basketball prediction and analytics

For the past couple of years, a hobby of mine has been writing code to predict the outcomes of college basketball games; this evolved partly as an exercise to teach myself some Python and machine learning techniques, and partly as a new way to follow my favorite sport. For the upcoming 2015-2016 season, I'll be posting regular-season game predictions here. I'll also be submitting these picks to the Prediction Tracker, where you can follow my performance. I did quite well last year (submitting under the moniker "ERFunctionRatings"), though my performance was somewhat inflated since I started submitting picks a bit later in the season. This year I plan to begin submitting picks after ~3 games into the season.
